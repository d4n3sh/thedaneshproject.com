YouTorrent the new torrent search engine

YouTorrent is the latest torrent search engine to hit the net. What’s different this time is that YouTorrent’s searches are real-time, meaning that no torrents files are hosted by YouTorrent but instead it goes out and graps meta information from other popular torrent search engines out there. YouTorrent currently works with Mininova, The Pirate Bay, IsoHunt, MyBitTorrent, NewTorrents, SuprNova, Monova, Vuze, BitTorrent, LegitTorrents, SeedPeer , BT Junkie, SumoTorrent.
